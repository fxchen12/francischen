MicroChain is a web application for philanthropic microlending. It uses Ethereum's blockchain to create an inalterable activity history, facilitating trust and fraud detection. We developed it for Professor Ron Rivest's Network and Computer Security class at MIT.

The Wind and the Tide is my first album. It tells stories from my time as an undergraduate at MIT. I composed all 7 songs in 2012-2014. I performed the whole album, including piano, guitar, and vocals, to produce this studio recording in 2015.

Days of the Summer was inspired by my time in Fairfield, CT during the summer of 2014. I composed it as a senior at MIT, for the Harmony and Counterpoint II class. The piece uses approaches from Western Classical music.
